"Falling from High Places" out now!

03/21/2025

Bad Optics returns with the song Falling from High Places, the first single from their forthcoming record The Bounded World. The song is a jaunty romp through the bowels of love, featuring the band's signature twin-guitar attack and paranoiac vocals taken a step further into pure mania. Rapid-fire chord progressions slip in and out of each other like a malfunctioning piston, pausing only momentarily during the song's bell-inflected choruses. Fans of time-signature-shifting and key-signature-ignoring will be delighted. Lyrically, the song takes inspiration from 19th-century romantic literature, and it will provide the perfect soundtrack for a relaxing night in with your sweetheart and/or pet of choice.

Accompanying the release is a new music video, which was directed, shot, & edited by the band. Matching the quick-change style of the song, it is a surrealist stew of images featuring faceless ghouls performing senseless tasks.

Bad Optics new record *The Bounded World* will be out April 30th via Panopticon Recordings and Den Tapes.

New album "The Bounded World" out April 30th

03/07/2025

Pull yourself up from the muck, little blind cave salamanders, and rejoice! Bad Optics returns with THE BOUNDED WORLD, a brand new full-length album, a 12-part salve for the psykick wounds inflicted by the absurd hellworld in which we all live. Do you ever wonder, WHAT is the worth of a man? Have you ever asked, IS everything getting worse? Do you find yourself running nude into the night, crying to the heavens in vain, wondering WHERE is the intersection of form and function? Then fear not, friends, for we will provide the answers to these questions and more.

Taking inspiration from Christian millenarian cults, "rationalist" tech freaks, The Picture of Dorian Gray, and mid-century-modern furniture, this new record will be released on April 30th, 2025. In addition to their usual cacophonous twin-guitar sound and paranoid songwriting, Bad Optics now features MORE melodies, MORE vocal harmonies, MORE synthesizers, and a glockenspiel. Get ready for fun!

Lead single Falling from High Places will be released on March 21st along with an accompanying music video. A jaunty romp through the bowels of love, this song can provide the perfect soundtrack for a relaxing night in with your sweetheart and/or pet of choice.
