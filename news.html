New album "The Bounded World" out April 30th, tour forthcoming

03/07/2025

Exciting news, little blind cave salamanders! We have a new album coming out on Wednesday, April 30th, in the year of our lord 2025. We've been working on this for ALMOST A YEAR. That's the power of DIY rock music, with which you too can try to balance producing a full-length record and playing shows whenever possible with working a full-time job. It's magical!

Not to bury the lead there, but it is indeed a full-length (read: long play) album. Enrobed with the moniker THE BOUNDED WORLD, this stereophonic recording contains 12 songs of pure maximalist bliss. We've been writing it since sometime around early 2023, so you know it's just been stewin' and marinatin' and a-quiverin' with flavor. There are several differences you may notice from our previous releases, the main one being the overwhelming cacophony of synthesizers. Don't worry, this is completely intentional - we did not fall asleep atop a Moog in the studio, nor are we getting into the dreaded idea of creating "soundscapes". You'll still find ear-piercing, heavy guitars accompanied by a man nervously shouting at you, so don't worry. You may also notice that this record contains MORE melodies, MORE vocal harmonies, and MORE unnecessary chord changes. Get ready for fun.

Inspired by millenarian cults, "rationalist" tech freaks, The Picture of Dorian Gray, and mid-century furniture, the album paints an uneasy picture of a world filled with more problems than solutions. Should we all give up and start sacrificing ourselves to the Kinich Ahau, the Mayan sun god? Bad Optics says, unequivocally, yes. Start building those ziggurats.

The first single from the album is called Falling from High Places, which will be out on March 21st along with a music video. We'll also be announcing West Coast tour dates for early May soon, as well as a release show in Seattle once we're back. Clamp your eyelids open, A Clockwork Orange-style, and check back soon for that info.
